Run Generate.bat to generate the CDMs from the Models and textures (may take a while)

Note: Textures have to resized to 512 x 512 to save space and converted to TGA format for compatibility

Run GameEngine.exe to view the models in the engine.

Controls:

Home - Start Orbit;
End - Stop Orbi;

W - move in look at direction.
S - move opposite look at direction.

up - move up.
down - move down.
left - turn left.
right - turn right.

A & D - move in X axis;
R & F - move in Z axis;
